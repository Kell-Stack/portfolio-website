About Me

My name is Isabelle Yiu. I am a local journalist making the transition into software engineering. As a journalist and editor for a daily newspaper in the past 6 years, I made it my mission to help the Chinese immigrant community by writing about social and political injustices in San Francisco.

Being a reporter in San Francisco, technology was a constant topic and I saw how empowering technology can be. I realized I can be more than just a voice for the issues that I had been writing about. I can try to be part of the solution.

I am currently a Software Engineering Apprentice at Techtonica, a non-profit apprenticeship program that provide full-stack JavaScript web development training for women and non-binary adults. My goal is to make applications that would benefit the lives of other low income immigrants.
